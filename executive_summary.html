Team Growth

Blue Whale grows from a mono-school all-male team to a group with students coming from 14 schools in 5 cities, ranging from 3 countries. Now 48% of the team members are female. Number of mentors grows from 2 to 5.

Alumni Network

All alumni have frequent contact with the team. Many alumni have gone into a STEM field in foreign universities.

Mission Statement

Inspired by FIRST (For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology), the motto of Blue Whale is WHALE (With the Highest Ambition and Love for Endeavors). Our mission is to create a safe and exciting environment for students to foster and develop their interests in robotics, STEM, and other fields concerned.
